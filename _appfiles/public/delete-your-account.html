Delete Account from NewsCrisp by softdot

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our app, please carefully follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and click on the user icon in the top right corner of the screen.
  2. From the dropdown menu, select "Delete Your Account".
  3. You will be shown a confirmation screen asking you to confirm account deletion.
  4. Read the information on data removal and select "Confirm Deletion" to permanently delete your account.

By deleting your account, all your associated personal data will be removed from our servers and databases and your account will no longer be accessible.

Please note that deletion of your account is permanent and irreversible. Any content you have posted or data associated with your account will not be recoverable once the account is deleted.

If you have any other questions, please contact our support team before initiating account deletion.
